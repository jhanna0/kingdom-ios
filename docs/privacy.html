Privacy Policy

Quick Summary: We collect minimal data to run the game. Your location data stays on your device. We use Apple Sign In for secure authentication. We don't sell your data to anyone.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Kingdoms ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Apple Sign In: When you sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple User ID and optionally your name and email address
  • Display Name: Your chosen username in the game
  • Avatar: Optional profile picture

2.2 Game Data

  • Gameplay Statistics: Gold, level, experience points, reputation, honor
  • Game Actions: Check-ins, kingdom claims, contracts, alliances, battles
  • In-Game Communications: Chat messages sent in town pubs and direct messages

2.3 Location Information

  • How We Use It: Location is used to determine which kingdoms you can interact with based on proximity
  • Storage: Your precise location coordinates are NOT stored on our servers
  • What We Store: Only kingdom check-in events (which kingdom you visited and when)
  • Control: You can disable location access at any time in iOS Settings, but this will limit game functionality

2.4 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version
  • Usage Data: App crashes, performance metrics (via AWS CloudWatch)
  • Login Activity: Last login timestamp

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and operate the game services
  • Create and manage your player account
  • Enable location-based gameplay features
  • Facilitate in-game interactions with other players
  • Send game notifications (conquests, battles, events)
  • Improve game balance and fix bugs
  • Prevent cheating and enforce terms of service
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 What We DON'T Do

  • ‚ùå We do NOT sell your personal information
  • ‚ùå We do NOT share your location data with third parties
  • ‚ùå We do NOT use your data for advertising

4.2 What We DO Share

  • Other Players: Your display name, avatar, level, and in-game actions are visible to other players
  • Service Providers: AWS (database and API hosting) has access to data for infrastructure purposes only
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights and safety

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Infrastructure: Data is stored on AWS (Amazon Web Services) in US data centers
  • Encryption: All data transmission uses HTTPS/WSS encryption
  • Passwords: Not applicable - we use Apple Sign In (no passwords stored)
  • Access Control: Only authorized systems can access the database

6. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Your data is retained while your account is active
  • Inactive Accounts: Accounts inactive for 2+ years may be deleted
  • Deleted Accounts: Account data is permanently deleted within 30 days of deletion request
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update incorrect or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request account deletion (contact support)
  • Opt-Out: Disable location services or notifications in iOS Settings
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format

8. Children's Privacy

Kingdoms is rated 12+ and is not intended for children under 12. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 12. If you believe a child under 12 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. International Users

Our services are hosted in the United States. If you are accessing from outside the US, please be aware that your information will be transferred to, stored, and processed in the US.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the app or via email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
