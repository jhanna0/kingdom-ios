How do I claim a kingdom?

Walk to a kingdom's location on the map and tap "Claim Kingdom" when you're within range. The first player to claim an unclaimed kingdom becomes its ruler!

Why can't I check in to a kingdom?

Make sure you're within the kingdom's boundary (shown as a colored territory on the map) and you haven't checked in recently. There's a cooldown period between check-ins.

How do alliances work?

Rulers can propose alliances to other kingdoms. Allied kingdoms can support each other in battles and share resources. Break alliances carefully - it affects your reputation!

What are coups and invasions?

Coups allow subjects to overthrow their ruler, while invasions let rulers attack other kingdoms. Both require strategic planning and resources. Check the in-game help for details.

How do I earn gold and experience?

Check in to kingdoms regularly, complete construction contracts, participate in group hunts, and engage in battles. Higher-level activities reward more gold and XP!

My location isn't working

Go to iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services. Make sure Location Services are enabled and "Kingdoms" is set to "While Using the App" or "Always".

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings in the app and select "Delete Account". This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your progress will be lost.

Can I play without giving location access?

Kingdoms is a location-based game, so most features require location access. However, you can still view the map and see game events without enabling location.
